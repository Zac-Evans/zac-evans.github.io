A History of Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic the Hedgehog was conceived by Sega as their offical mascot- a true rival to Nintendo's ever popular mascot, Mario. With the release of the titular Sonic the Hedgehog game in 1991, Mario immediately had competiton that was perceived as edgier, faster, and more inline the 90s aesthetic of "radness." However, where Mario's games have maintained a high threshold of quality over the years, Sonic games have ranged from passable to abysmal.

Sonic games have included a range of ill-conceived characters including:

  • A giant dumb cat with a pet frog
  • An edgy and dark antagonist hedgehog that wields a gun
  • Another hedgehog that looks slightly different, is silver, and is actually named Silver
